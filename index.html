Investment Potential

Why Invest in Hoskote, East Bangalore?

Hoskote is rapidly emerging as the most lucrative real estate growth corridor in Bangalore. Historically known as an industrial and logistics hub, the region is currently undergoing a massive transformation driven by unprecedented government infrastructure spending. The development of the Satellite Town Ring Road (STRR) is an absolute game-changer, designed to connect Hoskote directly to key zones of Bangalore without passing through the congested city center. Read our in-depth guide: Why Hoskote is Bangalore's Next Real Estate Hotspot →

Proximity is power in real estate. Sobha One World is strategically positioned just minutes away from Greater Whitefield and the bustling ITPL (International Tech Park Bangalore). This ensures that the thousands of IT professionals working in East Bangalore have a premium, short-commute residential option. Furthermore, the extension of the Namma Metro Purple Line up to Kadugodi has drastically reduced travel times to the CBD (Central Business District).

From an investment standpoint, early-mover advantage in a mega-township of this scale guarantees significant capital appreciation. Properties in Hoskote are currently priced highly competitively compared to the saturated Whitefield market, yet they promise similar, if not better, future returns and high rental yields. With Kempegowda International Airport a smooth 40-minute drive away via the cargo road, Hoskote is undeniably the future of Bangalore's luxury real estate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries regarding the development, location, pricing, and possession timelines.

What exactly is the Sobha One World Hoskote project?
Sobha One World is an ultra-premium, ~300-acre master-planned residential township developed by Sobha Limited. Located in the rapidly growing corridor of Hoskote, East Bangalore, it represents one of the largest and most luxurious residential launches in the city. The project will feature multiple high-rise towers containing thousands of premium 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments, complemented by over 50+ world-class amenities and 70% open green spaces.
Where is the project located and how is the connectivity?
The project is strategically positioned in Sarakariguttahalli, Hoskote, adjacent to the Old Madras Road and near Greater Whitefield. Connectivity is exceptional: it is merely 5 minutes from National Highway 75 and lies directly adjacent to the upcoming Satellite Town Ring Road (STRR). Residents will enjoy a seamless, traffic-free commute to the major IT hubs of Whitefield and ITPL, while the Kempegowda International Airport is easily accessible within 40 minutes via the cargo road. See our full Connectivity Guide: Hoskote to Whitefield & Airport →
What is the starting price and what configurations are available?
The pricing for Sobha One World is highly competitive for the luxury segment it occupies. Prices start from ₹1.09 Cr* onwards for a spacious 1 BHK residence (approx. 734 sq.ft.). The project also offers expansive 2 BHK, 3 BHK Luxe, and 4 BHK Grande configurations ranging up to 2,650 sq.ft. Please register your interest via the inquiry form to receive the most up-to-date, detailed pricing and payment plans.
What is the current RERA status and expected possession date?
As a newly announced pre-launch project, the official RERA application for Phase 1 (Sobha One Residences) is currently under process with the Karnataka Real Estate Regulatory Authority. The official possession timelines and delivery schedules will be formally announced alongside the receipt of the RERA registration number. Sobha Limited is renowned for its strict adherence to delivery timelines.
Is Hoskote a good location for real estate investment?
Absolutely. Hoskote is frequently cited by real estate experts as Bangalore's next big growth frontier. With saturated markets in central Whitefield and skyrocketing prices, Hoskote offers a rare combination of affordability, massive infrastructure infusion (STRR, Metro extension), and proximity to massive employment hubs. Investing in a reputed Grade-A developer project like Sobha One World here guarantees substantial capital appreciation and strong rental yields over the next 5-10 years.
